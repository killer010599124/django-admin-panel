Sights in Leipzig Exhibition centers and hotels in Leipzig
With around 520,000 inhabitants, Leipzig is one of the three largest cities in East Germany. With a history going back more than 800 years, the Leipzig Trade Fair is one of the oldest trade fairs in the world. The fair has a total of five interconnected exhibition halls. This results in an area of ​​102,500 m². Next to the Congress Center Leipzig there is another glass hall with various service facilities that has been awarded for its creativity. There are also some 70,000 m² of outdoor space. Due to the good signage, the fair is easy to reach by car. Sufficient parking spaces are available for visitors. The exhibition center can also be reached from the main station in six minutes as quickly as possible. There are also numerous hotels in all price categories near the main train station. Hotels at the fair are also available. Major trade fairs are INTERSCHUTZ, the leading trade fair for rescue, fire/disaster control and security, the Leipzig Book Fair and MUTEC, the trade fair for museum and exhibition technology.
